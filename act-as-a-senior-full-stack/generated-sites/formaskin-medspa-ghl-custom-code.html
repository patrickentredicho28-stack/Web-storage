Before you book

Questions that should be answered early.

Do I need to know the exact treatment?

No. You can start with the result you want, then your provider will help match the right route.

What if I have an event soon?

We plan around timing first so you avoid swelling, peeling, or recovery windows that do not fit.

Can I keep the result subtle?

Yes. Natural movement, facial balance, and conservative sequencing guide the recommendation.

Do I have to treat the same day?

No. You can leave with a plan and book when the timing, budget, and comfort level are right.
